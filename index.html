Features

Track

Track the top 100 crypto of the moment. Each cryptocurrency has it's price, market cap and 24h change listed. Thus making it easy to spot which crypto are performing well and which ones are not.

Favorite

You can add cryptocurrencies to your favorites. Once added they will be dipslayed in a seperate window. This makes it really easy to follow up on the cryptocurrencies you think are most important.

Share

With the share feature you can easily share the most important info of a currency online. Upon clicking the share button you'll be able to choose which platform to share on.
